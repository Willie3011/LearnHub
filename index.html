Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

To enroll in a course, find the course you're interested in and click the "Enroll" button. You'll be prompted to sign up or log in, and then you can complete the enrollment process.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied with a course?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for most courses. If you're not satisfied with a course, contact our support team within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.

Do I need any prerequisites to take a course?

Some courses may have prerequisites, such as prior knowledge or experience in a particular subject. Check the course description for any listed prerequisites before enrolling.
